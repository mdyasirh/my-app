# Sample Test Images

To test LocalROSA, you need side-view photos of people sitting at computer workstations.

## How to Create Test Images

### Test Case 1: Perfect Posture (Expected ROSA: 2-3)
- Person sitting with knees at 90°, feet flat on floor
- Elbows at 90° on armrests, shoulders relaxed
- Back reclined at ~100° with lumbar support
- Monitor at eye level, arm's length away

### Test Case 2: Poor Chair Setup (Expected ROSA: 6-7)
- Seat too high (knees at ~70°), feet barely touching floor
- No armrests, slouching forward
- No lumbar support, back at 80°

### Test Case 3: Monitor Too Low (Expected ROSA: 4-5)
- Good chair posture
- Laptop on desk (screen too low, neck flexion ~30°)
- No external keyboard

### Test Case 4: Reaching for Mouse (Expected ROSA: 5-6)
- Moderate chair posture
- Mouse placed far to the right
- Wrist extended while typing

### Test Case 5: Phone Cradling (Expected ROSA: 6-8)
- OK chair posture
- Phone cradled between neck and shoulder
- Typing while on phone

### Test Case 6: Standing Desk (Low Position) (Expected ROSA: 3-4)
- Person sitting at adjustable desk
- Good overall posture
- Slight wrist extension

### Test Case 7: Everything Wrong (Expected ROSA: 8-10)
- Feet dangling, no armrests
- Hunched forward, no back support
- Monitor too far and too low
- Mouse reaching, wrist deviation

### Test Case 8: Moderate Issues (Expected ROSA: 4-5)
- Slight knee angle deviation (~105°)
- Armrests slightly too high
- Minor neck flexion (~15°)

## Photo Tips
- Take photos from directly to the side (90° angle)
- Ensure full body is visible (head to feet)
- Good, even lighting
- No obstructions between camera and subject
- Resolution: at least 640x480
